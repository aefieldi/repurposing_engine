The Content Alchemy system: One video or brain dump → 7 standalone short-form ideas → re-recorded clips → the right CTA for each clip type → new community members. Repeat weekly.
1
Record your YouTube long-form or do a brain dump — this is your content mine. Every short-form idea gets extracted from here. A 20-min video can fuel an entire week of content.
2
Get your transcript — YouTube → three dots → "Show transcript" → copy all. Raw auto-captions work fine. For voice memos, use Otter.ai or Descript to auto-transcribe.
3
Use the Content Briefs tab — fill in your setup, hit Build My Prompt, copy it, paste into a fresh Claude chat with your transcript underneath. Claude mines the content for 7 standalone clip opportunities and writes each brief as a starting point — not a script.
4
Re-record each clip natively — never cut from the YouTube video. Each brief gives you the core idea, a hook, bullet points of what to cover, and cold-audience framing notes. Show up on camera and make it your own. Re-records massively outperform cuts on short-form platforms in 2026.
5
Use the right CTA for each clip type — not every clip should have a comment keyword. Conversion clips get comment-to-DM. Story and hot-take clips get an engagement question. Pure reach/Discovery clips get a follow. Forcing a keyword CTA onto the wrong clip type hurts performance.
6
Set up your DM sequence before you post — use the DM Sequence tab. Load the sequence into ManyChat so it fires automatically when someone comments. On TikTok, ask people to DM you the keyword directly — comment-to-DM automation is Instagram only.
7
Stack the content ecosystem — Caption Writer for paste-ready captions, Blog Post for Google traffic, Email Blast to notify your list. One video, seven content touchpoints, zero overwhelm.
✦
Auto-distribute everywhere at once — Repurpose.io connects YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn and more so one upload goes everywhere automatically.
Tier 1 — Comment keyword → DM: Only use when the clip is directly relevant to something you can send them — a free resource, the full video, or your community. The value exchange has to feel obvious and earned. This is where conversions happen.

Tier 2 — Engagement question: For hot takes, personal stories, and standalone insights that don't naturally connect to an offer. Asking a genuine question ("Does this resonate with you?" or "Which stage are you in?") builds comment count, trains the algorithm, and deepens connection — without feeling forced.

Tier 3 — Follow for more: For pure Discovery clips — pattern interrupts, broad contrarian takes, wide-appeal content. The only ask is the follow. Maximises reach signal and builds the audience that converts later.
The tool extracts ideas and gives you a starting point — not a word-for-word script. The best short-form content is delivered fresh, in your own energy, in the moment. A brief tells you what the clip is about, what hook to open with, what points to hit, and how to frame it for a cold audience. You show up and make it yours. That's what performs.
Instagram capped all posts at 5 hashtags in December 2025. TikTok recommends 3–5. Neither platform uses hashtags for reach anymore — they're purely a search and categorisation signal. The energy you used to put into hashtag research is better spent on keyword-rich captions and spoken words in the video itself, since both platforms now index audio and on-screen text for search.
