The Content Alchemy system: One YouTube video → 7 production-ready short-form briefs → a full week of content → a DM pipeline → new community members. Repeat weekly.
1
Record your YouTube long-form video — this is your content hub. Every other piece of content repurposes from here.
2
Get your transcript — YouTube video → three dots below → "Show transcript" → copy all. Raw auto-captions work perfectly, no cleanup needed.
3
Run the Find Clips tab — the tool searches for trending hashtags in your niche first, then generates 7 full production briefs: spoken hook, text-on-screen specs, b-roll shot list, pacing notes, comment keyword with rationale, caption, and real hashtags.
4
Re-record each clip as a standalone video — NOT cuts from the YouTube video. Each brief tells you exactly what to say, what to show, and what b-roll to film. Re-records perform significantly better on short-form platforms because they are built for that energy and pacing.
5
Post with the 2-step CTA — end every clip with "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you the full training." On TikTok say "DM me [KEYWORD]" — comment-to-DM automation is Instagram only via ManyChat.
6
DM everyone who engages — use the DM Sequence tab to write your 3-part message flow in advance so you are ready to respond instantly.
7
Stack the content ecosystem — Blog Post tab for Google traffic, Email Blast tab to notify your list. One video, seven content touchpoints.
✦
Want to auto-distribute all 7 clips across every platform at once? — Repurpose.io connects your YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn and more so one upload goes everywhere automatically. It is the tool that makes this whole system truly hands-off.
In 2026 the algorithm shows your content to existing followers first — if it performs there, it goes wider. Re-recorded clips are built for short-form pacing, energy, and context. Clips cut from a YouTube video carry YouTube energy and consistently underperform on Reels and TikTok.
